About the App

Craft Brewery Finder helps you discover local breweries, explore by brewery type, and save your favorites for future visits. Whether you're planning a road trip or looking for somewhere new in your neighborhood, we'll help you find your next great taproom.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for breweries?

Use the search feature to find breweries by location or filter by brewery type (brewpub, microbrewery, taproom, etc.).

How do I save a brewery to my favorites?

Tap the heart icon on any brewery listing to add it to your saved list. Access your favorites anytime from the Favorites tab.

The app isn't showing breweries in my area. What should I do?

Make sure location services are enabled for Craft Brewery Finder in your device settings. If you're still having trouble, contact us and let us know your general location so we can investigate.

How do I report incorrect information about a brewery?

We want our listings to be accurate. Use the contact form below to let us know what needs updating.
