Craft Brewery Finder Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Overview

Craft Brewery Finder ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Location Data
With your permission, the App accesses your device's location to show breweries near you. This data is used only within the App to provide search results and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Favorites and Preferences
When you save breweries to your favorites, this information is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or sync this data to external servers.

Analytics
We may collect anonymous usage data (such as which features are used most frequently) to help us improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting support.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for maps and brewery data. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 21 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
